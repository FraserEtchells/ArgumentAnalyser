Argument Analyser

Welcome to Argument Analyser! This web-tool aims to break down the arguments in your persuasive essays and give you feedback based on them.

This is done by extracting the Argument Components in your essay, and basing criteria based on them. Argument Components are seen as:

Major Claims - The overall Thesis statements of your essay. They often appear in your introduction and conclusion.

Claims - The points made to justify your Major Claims. They essentially make up the sub-arguments within your essay. They often appear in your main body paragraphs.

Premises - The reasoning behind your Claims. They provide context and justifications. Several of these often appear in your main body paragraphs.
